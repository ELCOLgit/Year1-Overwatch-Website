Esports

Overview

The 2022 Overwatch League postseason takes place from Oct. 14–Nov. 4, with the online regional Countdown Cup play-ins leading into a double-elimination playoff bracket, culminating in the first in-person Grand Finals in three years. This year the Overwatch League is thrilled to welcome fans to the Anaheim Convention Center—home of BlizzCon —to see the top teams from both regions compete on the same stage for the ultimate title. There’s US $2.55 million at stake in the playoffs.
